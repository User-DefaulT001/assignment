Bangers and Mash

Bangers and Mash: A traditional British dish of sausages served with creamy mashed potatoes, often topped with onion gravy and accompanied by peas.

Beef Wellington

A luxurious dish consisting of a fillet of beef coated with pâté and duxelles (a finely chopped mushroom mixture), wrapped in puff pastry, and baked until golden.

Classic English Shepherd's Pie

A comforting dish made with minced lamb (or beef in the variant called cottage pie) cooked with onions, carrots, and peas, topped with mashed potatoes, and baked until golden.

Fish and Chips

A classic British dish featuring battered and deep-fried fish (typically cod or haddock) served with thick-cut fries, often accompanied by mushy peas and tartar sauce.

Full English Breakfast

A hearty breakfast consisting of bacon, sausages, eggs, baked beans, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and toast, often served with black pudding and hash browns.
