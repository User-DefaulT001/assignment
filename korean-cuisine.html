Bibimbap

A vibrant Korean rice dish topped with sautéed vegetables, marinated beef, a fried egg, and spicy gochujang sauce, all mixed together for a flavorful and colorful meal.

Korean Cold Noodle Soup (Mul Naengmyeon)

Mul Naengmyeon is a Korean cold noodle soup featuring chewy noodles in a tangy, chilled broth, garnished with fresh cucumber, sweet pear, pickled radish, and optional beef slices. It's a perfect, refreshing dish for hot weather.

Spicy Carrot Kimchi

Spicy Carrot Kimchi is a tangy Korean side dish made from carrots coated in a spicy, garlicky paste and fermented for a few days. It's a crunchy, flavorful addition to any meal.

Kimchi Bokumbap: Kimchi Fried Rice

Kimchi Bokkeumbap is a flavorful Korean fried rice made with tangy kimchi, day-old rice, and a mix of vegetables and protein, often topped with a fried egg.

Korean Rice Cake Soup (Duk Guk)

Duk Guk is a traditional Korean rice cake soup made with thinly sliced rice cakes in a clear beef or anchovy broth, often garnished with beef, green onions, and egg. It's commonly enjoyed during the Lunar New Year for good luck and to symbolize aging by a year.
